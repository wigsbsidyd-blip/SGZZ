Not only that, he is also learning FL Studio under the guidance of Kurfaat, which is helping him sharpen his production skills and shape his musical journey.

And when he’s not making music? SGZZ! is known for his fun personality — sometimes even raiding online classes with his friends, showing the lively and playful side behind the beats!
